Frequently Asked Questions

What are cohort rosters?

All students enrolled in the Kaplan program are activated by the submission of rosters to your school’s dedicated Account Manager. The rosters tell us which students to activate and classify them into cohorts labeled by month and year of graduation (e.g. June 2017). Based on their cohort, students are granted access to the Kaplan program for three months after graduation, which typically covers their NCLEX® testing cycle. Kaplan can provide student extensions as needed beyond this standard access window.

How do I submit a new cohort roster?

To submit a new student roster download our New Class Roster Sheet, fill it out, and email it to your Account Manager at least 10 business days prior to the requested access start date. Kaplan will activate the student accounts based on the information in the Class Roster Form within 10 business days of receipt of the form. Students will not be able to access program materials until after the roster is received and processed by Kaplan.

Depending on your contract, your school may use a specific roster form that is different from the standard Class Roster Form. If a separate form has been provided to you by your Account Manager, please continue to use that form.

What happens after I submit a cohort roster?

As students are entered into the Kaplan system, they are emailed a user name and password that provides them with access to all the resources they will utilize in conjunction with the Kaplan program.

How do I submit a change or update?

To report any changes or updates in your rosters, submit them to your Account Manager using your Custom Change Form Custom Change Form at least 3 business days prior to your planned billing date. For urgent requests (needed within 3 business days), please contact the Faculty Hotline at 1-866-920-6311 or your Account Manager.
