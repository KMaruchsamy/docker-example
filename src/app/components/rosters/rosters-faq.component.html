Frequently Asked Questions

What are cohort rosters?

All students enrolled in the Kaplan program are activated by the submission of rosters to your school’s dedicated Account Manager . The rosters tell us which students to activate and classify them into cohorts labeled by month and year of graduation (e.g. June 2017). Based on their cohort, students are granted access to the Kaplan program for three months after graduation, which typically covers their NCLEX® testing cycle. Kaplan can provide student extensions as needed beyond this standard access window.

How do I submit a new cohort roster?

To submit a new student roster download our New Class Roster Sheet, fill it out, and email it to your Account Manager at least 10 business days prior to the requested access start date. Kaplan will activate the student accounts based on the information in the Class Roster Form within 10 business days of receipt of the form. Students will not be able to access program materials until after the roster is received and processed by Kaplan.

Depending on your contract, your school may use a specific roster form that is different from the standard Class Roster Form. If a separate form has been provided to you by your Account Manager, please continue to use that form.

What happens after I submit a cohort roster?

As students are entered into the Kaplan system, they are emailed a user name and password that provides them with access to all the resources they will utilize in conjunction with the Kaplan program.

How do I submit a change or update?

To submit a request for a roster change or update, click on the “Request change or update” link within the appropriate cohort roster. You can ask your Account Manager to make the following changes or updates:

  • Move a student to a different cohort or move a student from a different cohort to the current cohort (for example, if the student’s graduation date has changed).
  • Make a student in the current cohort inactive (for example, if the student has withdrawn for the semester or left the school).
  • Add a new student to the current cohort to enable access to Kaplan resources.
  • Request untimed versions of Kaplan tests for students in the cohort needing testing accommodations. Note: Students with testing accommodations are allowed to continue taking their test after the standard duration has passed. Once their test times down to zero, the student will see a window asking them to confirm they want to continue; upon clicking “OK”, their test will become untimed. It is up to the proctor to ensure that students taking untimed tests receive the correct amount of time for which they are eligible.
  • At the appropriate time, extend a student’s access to Kaplan resources beyond the standard access window (three months after the cohort’s graduation date, which typically covers their NCLEX® testing cycle). Access can be extended for another three months.

Please note: Cohort rosters will not immediately reflect requested changes or updates submitted through this process. Requests for changes or updates will be submitted to your Account Manager, who will process them within (2) business days and inform you via email when your changes have been processed. For urgent requests (needed within 3 business days), please contact the Faculty Hotline at 1-866-920-6311.
