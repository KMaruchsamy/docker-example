Academic Integrity and Test Security

You shall NOT:
  • Knowingly procure, provide, or accept any unauthorized or pirated Kaplan materials that contains question items or answers to any examination or assignment.
  • Assist any other person with obtaining or using unauthorized materials.
  • Provide students with unreleased or secure test items EXCEPT while administering an official Kaplan test. Using secure test items in any form at any time is a violation of test security.
  • Provide students with answers to any secure test items
  • Influence student response in any way to secure test items
You shall:
  • Administer Kaplan Secure Benchmark Tests in a secure, proctored setting.
  • Take all reasonable precautions to prevent cheating on any Kaplan test. You acknowledge that Secured Benchmark Tests are normed and that cheating impairs a valuable asset of Kaplan.
  • Promptly notify Kaplan if you become aware of any possible infringement of Kaplan’s ownership rights.

Kaplan shall promptly notify School Administration of any violation of this section and infringement of Kaplan’s ownership rights.

Additional Resources for Institutional Partners

Upon agreement to this Terms of Use, you will receive access to the Kaplan Nursing Integrated Testing Faculty User Guide. The Integrated Tests are intended to assist faculty and administrators in ascertaining students’ strengths and weaknesses in recalling and understanding nursing knowledge and in applying this knowledge in clinical situations. Test results enable nursing school faculty at the end of a program to identify a student’s knowledge and learning deficits in specific content area.

As noted in the guide, and in accordance with the industry’s Fair Testing standards, Kaplan Nursing recommends that the Integrated Tests not be used as the sole criteria for progression in or graduation from a nursing program, but considered one of several indicators of students’ accomplishments in basic nursing.
