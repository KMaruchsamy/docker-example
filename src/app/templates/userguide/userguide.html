Program Overview

Kaplan Nursing has been developing testing products and preparing nursing students to pass the NCLEX-RN® for more than 30 years. With Kaplan Nursing, you get experience and expertise in assessment testing. Kaplan’s first Integrated Testing Program was developed in 1999.

The Kaplan Integrated Testing Program is comprised of a series of online tests and remediation resources designed to evaluate and repair the nursing knowledge of students in an undergraduate program leading to RN licensure. The tests perform as end-of-course and end-of-program or exit evaluation tools for use during nursing school. In addition to testing basic nursing content, our tests evaluate students’ critical thinking skills. The results enable nursing school faculty to identify students with knowledge and learning deficits in specific content areas in a timely manner so that early intervention changes student outcomes.

The Integrated Testing Program also includes the Nursing Admission Test to assist nursing faculty during the admission process or to evaluate the basic skills of students already admitted to the nursing program. The Nursing Admission Test evaluates students’ reading, math, writing, and science knowledge and skills.

A comprehensive remediation program is included so early intervention improves student outcomes. Remediation is available 24 hours a day, seven days a week, in multiple formats so students can correct knowledge deficits.

The Kaplan Integrated Testing Program with NCLEX-RN® Review includes comprehensive preparation for the NCLEX-RN® exam. Students have access to individual accounts to practice answering questions like they will see during their actual exam. The student testing interface for these questions mirrors the NCLEX-RN® user interface in appearance and performance (i.e., students must answer a question and move forward, they cannot return to previous questions).

Students have access to two books (hard copy and/or eBook) when they start the Integrated Testing Program:

  1. The Kaplan Basics Book reviews nursing content in an easy-to-understand outline format with tables and graphs. It helps students identify important concepts about specific nursing topics so their textbook reading is targeted. It also helps students prepare for nursing school tests and Integrated Tests, or to remediate knowledge deficits after testing. Schools may use the index at the back of the book for specific topics.
  2. The Kaplan NCLEX® Content Review Guide reviews nursing content in sections as on the NCLEX® Test Plan, by Client Need Categories. It is also presented in an outline format. Complementary videos of each Client Need Category are available in the NCLEX® Prep resources.
