She has served in many roles as a nurse educator including Nursing Instructor and Director of Nursing in Practical Nursing and Registered Nursing programs. Presently, Maria is employed as the Senior Nurse Consultant for Kaplan Nursing and travels the country presenting workshops on nursing education topics such as test construction, test item writing, student success, critical thinking/clinical reasoning and NCLEX success.
